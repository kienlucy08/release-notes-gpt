**Deficiencies Attributes Bug: Missing Required Attributes for Processing**
Improve UX/UI | Data Processing

A bug where deficiencies added to Structure or Guy Facilities surveys during QC are missing essential attributes needed for processing, causing failures when certain TIA issues are encountered.
*Details:*  
- Deficiencies added during QC lack necessary attributes for processing
- Missing attributes such as tia_def_identifier and tia_identifier_label
- Issue caused by changes in TypeORM update affecting null and undefined values in queries
*Impact to Users:*  
- Processing failures may occur for deficiencies with specific TIA issues
- Users may experience issues when retrieving detailed deficiency information
- Processing deficiencies without all required attributes may lead to data inconsistencies
